Saarah Javed

About Me

Saarah Javed is the founder of INQLAB Creative Studio, where policy meets art and advocacy meets design. Rooted in global development and grassroots organizing, her work weaves research, storytelling, and investigations to translate complex political realities into compelling narratives grounded in social justice.
Her consultancy spans Washington, D.C. to Berlin, advising on advocacy strategy, accountability, and movement-building across sectors. She approaches design as experimentation and power-shifting practice, guided by Audre Lorde’s reminder that transformation requires more than the tools and systems we inherit.
